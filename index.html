Thank you for visiting our wedding website! We put this site together so we can share our story, help our guests find accommodations and provide directions to "The Mountaintop" - the site of the ceremony and reception. We are counting down the days to 9.10.16, and we can't wait to spend the weekend with all of you in Virginia!

  • 2004

    Jack + Brooke (very fittingly) met on a road trip from their respective schools in TN to VA during their freshman year of college.

  • June 2009

    A year out of college, Jack and Brooke reunited at Brooke’s brother’s wedding in California.

  • August 2009

    They started dating long-distance Virginia to Oregon the next month.

  • 2010

    Jack flew to Virginia to drive Brooke across country to live in Oregon permanently.

  • 2011

    Jack defended a masters and started a Phd program in geology, serving as a great tour guide for Brooke around Oregon.

  • 2012

    Costumes became nearly a weekly ritual, especially at grad school parties.

  • 2013

    After Brooke earned her design degree, She and Jack took a 3-week trip to Ecuador and the Amazon rainforest.

  • 2014

    Brooke + Jack drove across country again with pianos in tow to their new home in Portland (long-distance was finally over)!

  • 2015

    Jack proposed after (yet another) cross-distance trip from his field site in Arkansas.

  • 2016

    Jack and Brooke start a new chapter in Corvallis.

The Mountaintop

The ceremony and reception will take place a long 3 miles up the road from "The Barn" where Brooke's parents live.

Directions

Follow Lonesome Mountain Road to the end of the pavement and then continue up the gravel to the very top. Veer left towards "The Mountaintop" when you see Broadhead Cottage (which has the closest navigable address - 3950 Lonesome Mountaintop Rd)

Attire

Both the ceremony and reception will be held in a rustic setting where the ground may be uneven, so we ask that you please wear appropriate footwear (no heels!). Layers and attire for inclement weather might also be helpful. There is no official dress code, so wear what you wish!

Transportation

Transportation will be provided to and from the Hilton Garden Inn, the Comfort Inn and the English Inn. You will need to RSVP for a spot on the shuttle when you send in your RSVP for the wedding.
